Science, as a systematic endeavor grounded in empirical evidence and logical reasoning, is essential for understanding the natural world and driving technological innovation. It plays a critical role in healthcare, environmental stewardship, and informed decision-making, while fostering critical thinking and problem-solving skills. In our daily lives, science informs everything from cooking and transportation to healthcare and consumer choices, shaping the way we interact with the world and empowering us to make informed decisions for the betterment of society and the planet.

Energy transformations involve the conversion of mass into energy and the interplay between gravitational forces and the resulting potential and kinetic energies. Weight, as a measure of gravitational force acting on an object due to its mass, plays a key role in these transformations.

Atoms organize into molecules through chemical bonds, forming the fundamental building blocks of matter. This organization determines the properties and behavior of substances, influencing everything from the structure of materials to the functioning of biological systems.
